I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, advised by Prof. Qi Zhao. Our studies tackle the challenges by building computational models that, learn from human-like reasoning pattern, and clinical screening and CV.
